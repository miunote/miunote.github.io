By Miu 25 May 2021

使用:root及var來限制顏色使用範圍，全域變數(:root)設定為灰階色，區域變數(main)設定為藍色系，在排版的過程中只能使用設定好的顏色。

結構

樣式 :root{ –black-100: black; –black-90: #1E1E1E; –black-80: #353535; –black-70: #4E4E4E; –black-60: #696969; –black-50: #858585; –black-40: #A2A2A2; –black-30: #C0C0C0; –black-20: #DFDFDF; –black-10: #F6F6F6; –white-100: white; –space: 15px;
} … main{ –primary-default: #02A0E9; –primary-dark: #181677; –primary-tint: #C3E0F2; margin-bottom: calc(var(–space)*3);
}

瀏覽器支援 :root https://caniuse.com/?search=%3AROOT var https://caniuse.com/?search=var
